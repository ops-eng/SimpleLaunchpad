Quick Troubleshooting

  1. Open Settings and verify your apps are added correctly in Manage Apps.
  2. If the shortcut does not trigger, check macOS Accessibility permission.
  3. If Pro status is missing after reinstall, use Restore Purchases in the Pro tab.
  4. Restart the app after major settings changes if behavior seems stale.
