Privacy Policy

App: SimpleLaunchpad
Developer: Efthymios Tampouris
Effective date: April 20, 2026

SimpleLaunchpad is designed to work locally on your Mac. This policy explains what data is used and how.

Data Collection

SimpleLaunchpad does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data Stored on Device

The app stores settings locally on your Mac (for example: selected apps, layout preferences, shortcut configuration, and purchase-unlock state used by the app UI).

This local data is not sent to the developer.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not receive your payment details.

Permissions

Depending on your configuration, macOS may ask for Accessibility permission so the global shortcut can work reliably.

Third-Party Services

SimpleLaunchpad does not include third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact support through your project support page.
