AppShelf Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

Developer: Efthymios Tampouris

Overview

AppShelf is designed as a local-first productivity utility for macOS. It does not require account registration and does not run a developer backend for core functionality.

Data We Collect

AppShelf does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To function correctly, AppShelf stores app settings locally on your Mac, such as:

  • apps you added to your launcher
  • launcher layout preferences (columns, icon size, spacing)
  • shortcut preferences
  • local entitlement state used by the app UI

This local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

In-App Purchases

Pro unlock is processed by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. The developer does not receive your payment card or billing information.

Permissions

Depending on your setup, macOS Accessibility permission may be needed for global shortcut behavior. You can grant or revoke permissions in macOS System Settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

AppShelf does not include third-party analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking SDKs for core functionality.
