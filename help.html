AppShelf Help

Version: 1.0.0 (3)

What AppShelf Does

AppShelf is a lightweight macOS launcher that lets you open your selected apps from one clean grid. Instead of searching through Finder or Dock, you can keep a focused set of apps in a single launcher panel.

Core Features

  • Add apps you use most often.
  • Launch apps quickly from a visual grid.
  • Drag and drop to reorder apps.
  • Delete selected apps from your launcher list.
  • Optional launch-at-login support.
  • One-time Pro unlock for advanced customization.

Getting Started

  1. Open Settings from the menu bar icon.
  2. Go to Manage Apps and click Add App.
  3. Select apps from your Applications folder (or other locations).
  4. Reorder apps by dragging them in the grid/list.
  5. Use the launcher panel to open apps with one click.

Settings Tabs Overview

  • Manage Apps: add, reorder, and remove launcher entries.
  • Appearance: adjust columns, icon size, spacing, and idle hide delay (Pro).
  • Shortcut: configure activation shortcut modifiers and key (Pro).
  • Pro: purchase or restore the lifetime unlock.

Free vs Pro

Free: app limit and fixed defaults for some customization controls.

Pro (lifetime, non-consumable): unlock full appearance and shortcut customization, plus higher/no app limit.

Troubleshooting Shortcut

Shortcut changes apply immediately.

  1. Open System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility.
  2. Ensure AppShelf is listed and enabled.
  3. Check that your shortcut is not used by another app.
  4. Try a more unique key combination for testing.
